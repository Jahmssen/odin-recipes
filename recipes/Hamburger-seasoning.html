Stacey's Hamburger seasoning

This is what Stacey uses to season her hamburger patties before cooking them. It gives them a wonderful flavor that makes a so-so hamburger extraordinary.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 1/4 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper

Steps

    Step 1

  1. Combine the paprika, salt, pepper, brown sugar, garlic powder, onion powder, and cayenne pepper in an airtight container; seal. Shake to combine. Store in a cool, dry place between uses.
